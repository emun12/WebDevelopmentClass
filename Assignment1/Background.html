Poseidon the god of the Sea

This page will provide background information on Posiedon

Posiedon is one of twelve Olympian gods and goddesses. In Greek mythology, olympians were considered to be deities that affected/controlled major elements of the world. Some well known examples of these Olympians are Zeus, Aphrodite, and Ares.
Posiedon is one of the big 3 gods which include Zeus, Hades, and Poseidon. Between these three gods, different elements of earthquakes were split. Specifically, Zeus controlled the sky,Poseidon controlled the sea/ocean, and Hades controlled the underworld.
