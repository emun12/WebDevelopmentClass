Poseidon the god of the Sea

This page will specify Poseidon's unique powers

Similar to other greek gods and goddesses, Poseidon has super speed, super strength, immortality, and shapeshifiting abilities. His unique abilities are that he can control the sea and anything inside the sea like fish. He can control waves, storms, and winds. Further, he is known to control earthquakes, rivers, and other bodies of water. Besides having powers related to water, he is also known as the tamer of horses. Symbols of Poseidon such as horses, tridents, and fish can be attributed to his unique abilities.
